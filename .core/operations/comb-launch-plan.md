## Messaging Pillars

| Pillar | Core Message |
|--------|--------------|
| **Hook** | Your AI doesn't need a better summary. It needs a better memory. |
| **Lossless** | Nothing thrown away. Everything retrievable. Full text, always. |
| **Zero dependencies** | Pure Python stdlib. No server. No database. Copy the folder, copy the memory. |
| **Hash-chained integrity** | Tamper-evident conversation archive. Blockchain-grade history for AI agents. |
| **Honeycomb topology** | Three-directional graph: temporal, semantic, social. O(1) lookups. |
| **Open source** | MIT license. Built by Artifact Virtual. |

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Phase 1 blocks launch | Medium | Copy is ready; go-live can be delayed without rework |
| PyPI name conflict | Low | Reserve `comb-db` immediately |
| HN post gets no traction | Medium | Submit during peak hours (9–11am ET weekdays); engage genuinely in comments |
| Reddit post removed (self-promo) | Medium | Frame as technical showcase, not advertisement; personal account preferred |
| Low initial download count | Medium | Seed with internal use cases; document real-world examples |
